Transfer and School Placement Services
by Feiya Lin
Project Description
The brochure provides information about a full transfer service that helps students transfer across all levels of postsecondary education including transfers from Chinese to U.S. universities, from community colleges to four-year colleges, and from standard U.S. universities to top-50 universities. It was released on NAFSA EXPO 2017to establish WholeRen brand.
